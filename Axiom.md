# Definition
A statement or proposition that is regarded as being established, accepted, or self-evidently true. It serves as the starting point for further reasoning and arguments.
# Characteristics
- It is not proven (it's the foundation).
- It must be consistent (no contradictions).
- It should be independent (not derivable from other axioms).
# Examples
- [[Peano Axioms]] (for natural numbers)
- [[Euclidean Axioms]] (for geometry)
