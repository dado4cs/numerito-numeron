Bits representes one of two values, 0 or 1, and are joined together to form binary numbers. Each column of a binary number has twice the weight of the previous column, so binary numbers are *base 2*.
A group of eight bits is called a **byte**. It representes $2^8 = 256$ possibilitites.
A group of four bits, or a half a byte, is called a **nibble.**

The most significant bit (MSB) is the leftmost bit in a binary number. The least significant bit (LSB) is the rightmost bit in a binary numbero.
